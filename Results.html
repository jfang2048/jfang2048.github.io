All transition zone means are negative, confirming that pollutant concentrations declined regardless of land cover dynamics. PM₁₀ stable tree and tree gain zones show similar decreases (~ -1.65 µg/m³). For PM₂.₅, crops gain shows the strongest mean decrease. For NO₂, stable Built Area has the strongest mean decrease.
